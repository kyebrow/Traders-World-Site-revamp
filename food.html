Food

Center Stage

Located across from the stage on the Main Aisle.

Start the day off right with a healthy home-style breakfast of biscuits and gravy, scrambles eggs, and oatmeal. For lunch, treat yourself to the real old-fashioned Coney dog, brat, mett, pretzel, nachos, and/or taco salad. Also choose from a seasonally prepared entree menu of homemade chicken and dumplings, real mashed potatoes, pot roast, or Asian noodles. Maybe plan on warming up with Traders World famous Potato Soup.

The Chicken Shack

Located at the corner of main and Building 2.

Try our fresh, juicy, tender lightly breaded chicken served fresh all day long. Breast, wings, thighs, and legs sold a la carte. Order our signature potato wedges as a hearty side. You can try our loaded potato wedges, which include 8 wedges, chili con carne, and shredded cheddar cheese topped with sour cream. Our coleslaw is made fresh with a tangy, sweet sauce of freshly topped vegetables. You will not want to miss this healthy treat to complement your delicious chicken. Rolls and real butter top off every dinner. In true Jay Frick Sr. fashion, you will find yourself saying, “I have never had it so good.”On the sweet side, we are always serving Traders World signature chocolate chip cookies and oatmeal raisin cookies.

Pizza

Located next to The Chicken Shack in Building 2.

You’ll love Traders World pizza. We make our own special sauce and use only fresh ingredients made fresh for you. Try our delicious Pizza Subs put together with ham, salami, pizza sauce, garlic butter, and mozzarella cheese and served on a sub bun! Our Cheesy Bread Sticks come with four sticks with marinara dipping sauce. Our reuben pizza is a phenomenal blend of all the traditional sandwich ingredients that creates the “Worlds best Reuben Pizza.” Pizza can be bought by the slice or a whole pie. Ranch dressing is available with any pizza. Traders World Potato Wedges can also be ordered from this counter.

  • Traditional cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Deluxe: sausage, pepperoni, green pepper, onions, and mushrooms Bacon, onion, mushroom
  • Buffalo Chicken Pizza- chicken, hot sauce, cheese, ranch dressing, garlic butter

Sports Grill

Located near the big screen TV on the Main Aisle (center of the building).

The Traders World Sports Grill serves:

  • Funnel cakes with powdered sugar, cherry or apple topping
  • Chicken quesadilla
  • Corn dog
  • Chicken tenders
  • Chicken Tender Dinner
  • Fish sandwich
  • Wing dings (boneless wings) order a 6 piece or 10 pieces with hot sauce or BBQ
  • Mozzarella Cheese Sticks with marinara sauce
  • French fries
  • Onion Rings
  • Cole Slaw
  • Ranch Dressing

Enjoying these menu items while viewing your favorite sports team in our large seating area!

Ice Cream & Popcorn

Located at the corner of Main and Building 3.

Enjoy our delicious soft serve ice cream and The Double Dip’s homemade, hand-dipped ice cream and fresh popcorn. Ice cream can be served on a waffle cone, cake cone or inside a float. For your shopping convenience Traders offers a complete menus of hot dogs, sandwiches, chips, ice cream, and soda located in our South, North, and outside shelters. Lemon Freeze’s is also offered seasonally outside.
